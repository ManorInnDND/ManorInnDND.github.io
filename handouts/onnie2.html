Onnie - Boons of the Righteous

Another dream suggesting contact with Mt. Celestia occured last night, as you slept on the outskirts of The Labyrinth. The visiting angel had a warlike aspect and spoke in a stern paternal voice. As before the angels features are unclear, the brightness of it's form obscuring them from your eyes.

"Onnie Ottilie. There is discord in heaven, with some of our number unwilling to aid your realm against the terrible forces loosed upon it by foolish magic. I am not among them, and offer to widen the conduit connecting you to this place, therby expanding your capacity to return your home to a balanced, lawful state."

The figure kneels and fills a golden chalice with liquid from a alabaster fountain, then extends the chalice, brimming with sparkling clear liquid. Like water but thinner, like vodka but odorless.

"Drink and the arteries of your divine bloodline shall be succored."

You drink and dissolve into light.

What this means

You may now multiclass as warlock. Reskin warlock abilites to come from a divine source.

  • In general all damage you deal from warlock abilites is radiant damage.
  • You may not deal necrotic damage.
  • Xanathars guide to everything has a Celestial Warlock patron.
