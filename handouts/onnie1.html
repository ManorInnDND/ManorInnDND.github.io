Onnie - Prayers to Tymora

Last night you dreamt of a wonderfully good and ordered place, with lofty cloud-obscured mountains of cosmic magnitude. Brisk clean air wafts past, gently suggesting a presence. Turning from the range of mountains you see a lumnious angelic being, tall, handsome, and some. The being's face is obscure, as even your eyes are wearied by the refulgent halo emitted by this angel. She speaks, her voice hard and clear, and oddly materinal.

"Mount Celestia welcome a daughter home today, Onnie Ottilie." She pauses and turns towards the mountains behind you, before continuing.
