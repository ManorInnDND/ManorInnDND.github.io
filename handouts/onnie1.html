Onnie - Prayers of an Aasimar

Last night you prayed for assistance from your ancestors before drifiting off to sleep. You dreamt of a wonderfully good and ordered place, with lofty cloud-obscured mountains of cosmic magnitude. Soft steps on the snow approach, gently suggesting a presence. Turning from the range of mountains you see a lumnious angelic being, tall and vaguely feminine. The being's face is obscure, even your Aasimarian eyes are wearied by the refulgent halo above it. She speaks, her voice hard and clear, and... maternal?

"Mount Celestia welcome a daughter home today, Onnie Ottilie."

She pauses, turning towards the mountains behind you, before continuing. When you wake you try to recall all that she said, a few things stand out clearly.

"We are already acting against the Abyssal agents, Onnie, consider what you are and what you are doing..."
"In some time you will come to a place where traitors of my kind were long ago imprisoned. Take what you need from them and leave quickly, they deserve neither pity nor freedom."
"Our time together ends, you are needed on Faerun. Remain the light in the dark places, where all other lights go out."

Awake in the Tiny Hut, with the immensity of the Underdark looming down you feel something soft in your left hand hand, which turns out to be a magnificent downy feather about a foot long.

What this means

You are the help from Mount Celestia, stopping the demons is your destiny! So long as you possess the angel feather you gain the following racial abilities:

  • As a bonus action you grant yourself or an ally you can see advantage on any roll against a fiend. This ability can be used once per short rest.
  • As an action you expend the angel feather to restore yourself or an ally you can see to full hit points. The ally or yourself may have been dead for up to ten days. This does not resore spent spell slots or class abilites.
