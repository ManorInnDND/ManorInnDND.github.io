Onnie - A Harrowing Harvest

Another dream suggesting contact with Mt. Celestia occured last night, with your arrival at the Angel's Choir imminent. The same warlike angel visited you and warned you of the danger posed by the statues in the choir.

"Onnie Ottilie. My fallen brethren are restless within their stone cages. Beware as your draw close, for their minds remain powerfull and most of them will doubtless attempt to overpower the mind of any who draw near. See them now, and know their ways..."

A dim cavern appears with pools of water disturbed by droplets falling from overhead. 8 angel statues appear in the depths of the pools and tell you their stories. Upon awaking you only recall some gist of each story or experience:

An oracle who answered an impure questioner.

Prophet who foretold an unlawful future.

Manipulated mortals through dreams.

Repented too late.

Immoderately wrathful.

BAD MEMORY. Keep away.

Endless screams.

Attempted suicide; an unlawful act.

What this means

You have a vague idea of the danger the angel statues pose...
