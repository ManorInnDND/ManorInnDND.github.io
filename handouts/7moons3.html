Seven Moons - Ahm Reboot

You can think clearly again!

What this means

Whenever you have a form of indefinite madness Seven Moons reasserts himself as your persona. You also gain the abililty to cast the following spells based on your levels of madness. Also they stack so with two forms of indefinite madness you would be able to cast both disguise self and invisibilty 1/day each:

Levels of Madness Spells Available Frequency
1 Disguise Self 1/long rest
2 Invisibilty 1/long rest
