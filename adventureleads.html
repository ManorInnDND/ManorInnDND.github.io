Adventure Leads

Below are potential adventures you might pursue.

Ashabenford Area

Tasks near or in the town of Ashabenford are listed below:

Retrieve 3 Wyvern Stingers (200 gp)

Offered by Noristuor the Mage. You are to seek out the wyvern caves to the north of town and slay 3 of the beasts. Noristuor has promised you 200 sovereigns and may identify the 3 unusual books Zodius found in the haunted mansion.

Murder Drow (3 gp each)

Goliaoh, Captain of the local barracks has a standing offer for drow heads and ears. He will pay 3 gold per head or set of ears. Heads without ears will not be accepted.

Investigate the Drow Attacks (?)

Drow have killed and/or captured several townsfolk on the north side of the river. It is believed the attacks come from one of the innumerable passages within Gopher's Maw but so far no one has been able to track the source of the drow.

Miscellaneous Leads

Adventures not specific to one of the locales above can be found here:

Investigate the Cult of The Red Listen (?)

The Red Listen was the name of the cult that set you up in the Spoooky Mansion. Perhaps there are more of them doing evil in the world?

Deport Donamoth (?)

Donamoth, the demon who claimed vengeance against you for actions you supposedly took in a past life was defeated in his cave below the spoooky mansion. However his defeat seemed impernanent as he merely dissolved into black smoke. A sarcophagus in the cave contained the remains of a "Abn Bard Furgo", who sacrified himself along with six others to imprison some "great evil", presumably Donamoth. If Donamoth is not dead he may attempt to take vengeance on you again, though possibly not during this lifetime.
