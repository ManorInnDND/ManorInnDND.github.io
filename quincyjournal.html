Quincy's journal

Most of the journal is about Quincy's trouble with finding good help and complaints about an aging body. There are a few passages however that are interesting:

...I forgot the tune for the organ again and decided to earmark the page though guests aren’t as impressed when I have to go grab the book in the middle of dinner...
...Murdol has begun preparing my meals per my new regimen, the ingredients cost a fortune being magical so I’ll have him hide them in unassuming containers to keep my less trustworthy help put of trouble....

...My dreams have been most unpleasant of late, and all of my guests are suffering as well. Most have left. I dread going into the cellar though I am certain my latest purchase is to blame. I’ve not been bested by any tomb’s curse yet, and I won’t be now. I must have the coffin thoroughly disenchanted and then open the damn thing...

...I must find more information about the tomb from which the coffin came. I must visit Calimshan soon and see the tomb for myself. Unusual but Jeerko can surely be persuaded with some coin...

...I feel more apathetic about the coffin, perhaps I will leave it closes, the one foreign treasure I permit to remain inviolate...
