The Adventuring Party

A brief description of each member of the party follows.

Everin Godrick (Charlie)

Everin is a roguish half-elf, he is always lookin to make coin out of whatever he involved in. Not above pocketing valuables either.

Galifraen Amarondel (Luke)

Galifraen is an old high-elf accustomed to a nobles prestige. The actions of his fellow adventures often cause him to brood.

Onnie Ottilie (Sarah)

Onnie is a young Aasimar, she enjoys games of fortune and helping others.

Zodius Shadowtree (Brandon)

Zodius is an peculiar gnome, versed in the magical arts. He is often observed "pondering" his orb.

Seven-Stars (Brendon)

Seven-Stars is a tabaxi rogue, he steals food for food. He joined the party during their quest to recover wyvern stingers in Ashabenford during Session 5.

Shithead Dumas (Courtney)

Shithead is a repellent halfling, or perhaps even a goblin, his ugliness being dire enough to make racial assessments uncertain. He is young and prone to subjecting his foes to unpleasant humiliations. He joined the party during the escape from Sloobludop in Session 9.

Francis Madlok (Joshua)

Francis is a stern male human warrior in the prime of his life. He has said little of his past to his allies. He joined the party during the escape from Sloobludop in Session 9.
